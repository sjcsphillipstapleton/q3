Rose

An Alt-Rock-Pop-Synth-Punk Band

Origin

Rose is a band based in Greenville, SC. They formed from the roots of Insideout & Backwards, another band which had been playing the musical scene for about two years, after a breakup. Former IOB memebers include the lead singer Jacob Kiser, The lead guitarist Alex Hoverath, and the drummer Phillip Stapleton. New additions to the band include Micheal on the bass and Micheala Faye playing the keyboard/synth. This is an up and coming group in the Greenville area.

Usual Songs

Rose loves to play many cover songs whle alos writing their own music to perform. Clssic covers include: Mr. Brightside, Trogans, Laundry Room, and many more thrilling songs. Rose borrows from their roots and will sometimes play old IOB tunes such as Race to Nowhere or a Little Melody which always bring out some nostalgia. Rose's main point of focus currently is on writing new music that touches and expands the boundaries which they have so far reached. These new songs include: 18 Year Old Baby Girl and Lose More Sleep with many more in the works.
